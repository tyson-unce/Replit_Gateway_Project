Background of El Paso

El Paso is geographically the 7th biggest city in Texas and is known as The Sun City. While not the most known Texan city, it is undoubtly one of the most unique. El Paso is also a border city and the Mexican influence is very apparent in El Paso from the culture to the food. Also, you can actually visably see Mexico from El Paso.

El Paso was founded by Fray Garcia de San Francisco in 1659 and remains one of the most underappreciated states in Texas.
